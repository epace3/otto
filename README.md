# Otto Lite

**Your free starter pack of 10 real estate AI skills.**
Built for licensed agents. Install once, use forever.

Otto Lite is the free companion to the full **Otto** plugin — the senior real estate AI assistant for agents. If you like Otto Lite, the full Otto plugin runs complete listing, buyer, under-contract, and post-close packages in batch, saves everything into an organized workspace, and writes every email, post, and checklist in your voice.

---

## What's inside

Otto Lite ships with **10 skills** that work anywhere Claude runs — Cowork, Claude Code, or any Claude project.

### 5 Core Skills

1. **Humanizer** — rewrites stiff, AI-generated text so it sounds like a real person wrote it. Strips banned words, cuts hedging, matches your voice.
   *Trigger: "Humanize this: [paste]"*

2. **Market Researcher** — produces a structured market intelligence summary for any neighbourhood, property type, or price range. Gives you agent prep + client talking points in one pass.
   *Trigger: "Research the market for [area / price range]"*

3. **Objection Handler** — turns any buyer or seller objection into a 5-part response playbook (emotional driver, opener, core message, follow-up question, what NOT to say).
   *Trigger: "Handle this objection: [paste]"*

4. **Crucial Conversations** — helps you prep for the hard conversations you've been avoiding. Price reductions, angry clients, partner disagreements, firing a client.
   *Trigger: "Help me prepare for a difficult conversation about: [situation]"*

5. **Email Writer** — writes one-off client emails in your voice. Two subject lines, warm body, clear CTA. No more staring at a blank draft.
   *Trigger: "Write an email to [name] about [context]"*

### 2 Agent Modes

6. **Executive Assistant** — protects your time, runs your weekly priorities, extracts actions from meeting notes, keeps you focused on the activities that move your annual goal.
   *Trigger: "Be my executive assistant" or "What should I work on today?"*

7. **Listing Assistant** — handles everything listing-side from agreement signed to deal firmed up. MLS copy, feature sheets, social captions, seller emails, weekly updates, price-reduction prep.
   *Trigger: "Be my listing assistant" or "New listing — [address]"*

### 3 Workflows

8. **New Listing Launch** — intake → MLS description → feature sheet → social caption pack → seller intro email → photographer brief → open house promo. Seven deliverables in one pass.
   *Trigger: "New listing — [address], here are my notes: [notes]"*

9. **Weekly Listing Update** — showing activity summary, agent feedback rollup, market pulse, conditional price reduction prep, ready-to-send seller update email.
   *Trigger: "Weekly update — [address]"*

10. **Weekly Priorities** — gap analysis against your annual goal, time allocation, top 3 priorities, prospecting recommendation, the one thing you're avoiding. Designed to run every Monday.
    *Trigger: "Run my weekly priorities"*

## How to install

See `INSTALL_GUIDE.md` for step-by-step instructions on installing Otto Lite in Claude Cowork or Claude Code.

The short version:

1. Download the Otto Lite zip
2. In Cowork → Customize → Plugins → Browse → Add plugin from GitHub or zip
3. Paste the repo URL or upload the zip
4. Install Otto Lite
5. Start a new chat and trigger any skill with its trigger phrase

## What Otto Lite is NOT

Otto Lite is a **starter pack**. It gives you the 10 most-used skills for free. What it doesn't do:

- ❌ Batch-generate a full listing package (9 deliverables: welcome email, onboarding survey, pre-listing checklist, MLS description, feature sheet, launch social pack, open house promo, just-listed email, just-listed social)
- ❌ Batch-generate a full buyer package (6 deliverables)
- ❌ Run under-contract and post-close workflows
- ❌ Organize everything into a permanent `Otto Workspace/` folder structure with per-property subfolders
- ❌ Remember your profile, brokerage, voice, and sign-off across every conversation automatically
- ❌ Provide pre-loaded templates for 30+ common real estate emails, scripts, and social posts

For all of that, you want the **full Otto plugin**.
